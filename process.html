Franchise Onboarding Process

Becoming a Madrosc Pharma franchisee is easy. Follow these simple steps:

Step 1: Inquiry

Fill out the contact form or reach out to us via phone/email with your interest.

Step 2: Discussion

Our franchise advisor will contact you to explain terms, products, and territory availability.

Step 3: Document Verification

Submit your basic business documents like GST number and optional drug license.

Step 4: Territory Finalization

We confirm your district or zone for monopoly distribution and lock your franchise rights.

Step 5: Order Placement

You place your first minimum stock order and receive a starter pack with promotional material.

Step 6: Start Selling!

You’re officially a franchise partner of Madrosc Pharma. Begin distribution and grow with our support.
