Franchise Eligibility Criteria

Before applying, make sure you meet the following basic qualifications:

✔ Experience in Pharma or Sales

Candidates with experience in pharmaceutical sales, distribution, or retail are preferred.

✔ Valid GST Number

You must have a registered GST number to operate a franchise legally in India.

✔ Drug License (optional for some categories)

For certain product categories, a drug license is required. We can assist with guidance if needed.

✔ Financial Readiness

Must be able to meet the basic minimum order value and hold some inventory for local distribution.

If you meet these requirements, you're eligible to apply for a Madrosc Pharma franchise today.
